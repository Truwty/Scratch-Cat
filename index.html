About

SCRATCH
CAT

North London · Founded 2024

Scratch Cat are a four-piece alt-rock band from North London, tearing up stages and turning heads since their formation in 2024.

Fusing angular guitars, thunderous drums, pulsing bass and raw, urgent vocals — they carve out a sound that is both distinctly modern and deeply rooted in the best traditions of guitar music.
