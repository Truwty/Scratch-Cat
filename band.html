THE BAND

01 Lead Vocals · Lyrics · Composition

Stanley
Colson

Stanley is the voice, the pen, and the beating heart of Scratch Cat. As the band's primary lyricist and composer, he shapes the emotional core of every track — crafting words that hit like a fist and linger like a bruise. On stage, his raw energy and magnetic presence command every room the band plays.

Founded the band in 2024 with a clear vision: loud, honest, guitar-driven music with something to say.

📷 @stan.c7

02 Guitar

Fabio
Ingrey

Fabio is the sonic architect of Scratch Cat's sound, wielding his guitar with a mix of precision and ferocity that cuts through any crowd. His riff vocabulary pulls from the full spectrum of alt-rock — post-punk angles, indie jangle, and full-throttle noise — stitching it all into something unmistakably his own.

Whether shredding centrestage or locked in a groove with the rhythm section, Fabio's playing is the backbone everything else hangs off.

📷 @fabio.ingrey.music
